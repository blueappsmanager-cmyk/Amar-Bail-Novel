🔒 Privacy Policy

BlueApps ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app Amar Bail Novel handles your information.

By using this app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at blueappsmanager@gmail.com.

📲 App Features

Amar Bail Novel is a free digital reading app with the following features:

  • Read "Amar Bail" novel by Umera Ahmed
  • Bookmark pages for quick access
  • Jump to any page instantly
  • Resume reading from where you left off
  • Zoom mode for comfortable reading
  • Share Novel: Share this app with friends via social media
  • More Novels: Discover other novels by BlueApps

All features are provided free of charge. The Share and More Novels buttons use your device's native sharing capabilities and do not collect any data.

🛡️ No Data Collection

We believe in complete privacy. Amar Bail Novel does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

✓ No account creation required
✓ No personal details requested
✓ No data transmitted to our servers
✓ No tracking or analytics

💾 Local Storage Only

All app data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage feature. This includes:

  • Your current reading page
  • Bookmarked pages
  • Reading progress

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. Clearing your browser data will remove this information.

📱 Offline Usage

Amar Bail Novel works completely offline. Once the app is loaded, no internet connection is required to read the novel.

The PDF content is bundled within the app and does not require downloading from external sources after initial installation.

🔐 App Permissions

This app requires no special permissions to function:

  • No camera access
  • No microphone access
  • No location access
  • No contacts access
  • No storage write permission (uses internal browser storage)

↗️ Share Feature

The Share Novel button uses your device's native Web Share API to share the app link with friends. This feature:

  • Does not access your contacts list
  • Does not send data to our servers
  • Opens your device's default share menu only
  • Shares only the Play Store developer page link

📚 More Novels Feature

The More Novels button redirects you to our BlueApps Play Store developer page to discover other novels. This feature:

  • Opens the Google Play Store app or website
  • Does not collect any browsing data
  • Does not track your activity
  • Simply opens an external link to our developer page

🔗 Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that collect user data:

  • No Google Analytics
  • No Facebook SDK
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No external APIs

The only external resource loaded is PDF.js library from CDNJS for PDF rendering, which does not track users.

👶 Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. As no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.
